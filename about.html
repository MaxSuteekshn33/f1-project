The Story

You are probably wondering why a maths nerd but still a non-technical guy, an IITian with UG in Production & Industrial Engineering, with sheer love for creativity, and a 8.635 CGPA from IIT Roorkee is here making data analysis projects, writing and creating infographic and podcast series on Geopolitics and World History — with the occasional sprinkle of poetic philosophical write-ups — and managing work for HIPPOS as the founder.

"Not gonna lie, that is a very good question."
01
My love and passion for history — cemented more by preparation for the UPSC examination, though that dream didn't pan out as desired.
02
With UPSC out of the way, I took Naval Ravikant's advice to heart — "Find what feels like play to you but looks like work to others."
03
Maybe I am trying to figure out my Nika moment — that opportunity that comes along the way, or that I end up creating for myself.

So here we are — having dreamt of UPSC but not getting the results we desired — creating posts and analysis on Geopolitics, World History and Philosophy, firstly via blog format on Medium and LinkedIn, and now… making podcasts on YouTube and Instagram.

For someone who says he tends to stammer, I think I am doing a pretty good job creating those 1hr+ long livestreams on YouTube, discussing and engaging on Geopolitics.

"You need to be right only ONE time — but to get that ONE time you need to go out and do the hard bit." — Mark Cuban

If you have read this till here, you are a legend. I am Suteekshn Mahajan — an overthinker that has tried to convert his curiosity and problem-solving skills into a community of more than 6500 members across multiple platforms, talking and interacting on Geopolitics, World History and Philosophy.
